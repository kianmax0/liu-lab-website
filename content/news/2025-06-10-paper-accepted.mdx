Westlake University's official news platform published an in-depth feature on **Dr. Ding Liu** and his lab's research into the biology of loneliness.

The article, titled **"Westlake Scientist Ding Liu Explores the Landscape of Loneliness"**, covers the lab's approach to studying social need as a homeostatic drive — drawing parallels with hunger and thirst — and discusses the hypothalamic circuits identified in the lab's landmark *Nature* paper.

The piece highlights how the lab uses cutting-edge tools including calcium imaging, Neuropixels recordings, and automated behavioral tracking to probe the neural basis of social behavior in freely moving animals.
