We are proud to announce that **Dr. Ding Liu** has officially joined the School of Life Sciences at Westlake University, establishing the **Systems Social Neuroscience Lab**.

Dr. Liu brings a distinguished research background spanning Henan University (B.S.), the Chinese Academy of Sciences (Ph.D.), and Harvard University (Postdoctoral Fellow), where he made seminal contributions to understanding the hypothalamic circuits governing social homeostasis.

The lab pursues three interconnected research directions:

- **Social Homeostasis & Loneliness** — identifying the neural circuits that encode social need
- **The Power of Tactile Input** — exploring how touch modulates social behavior and satisfies social need
- **Neural Network Dynamics** — mapping population-level codes during naturalistic social interaction using Neuropixels and calcium imaging

The lab is actively recruiting Postdoctoral Fellows and Ph.D. students. Please visit the [Contact](/contact#openings) page for details.
