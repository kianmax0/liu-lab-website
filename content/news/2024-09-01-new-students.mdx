In February 2026, **Dr. Ding Liu** delivered a keynote address at the **CC Forum** hosted by the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS).

His talk, centered on _The Physiological Instincts of the Brain and Social Needs_, presented the lab's latest findings on:

- The homeostatic regulation of social drive by hypothalamic circuits
- How the brain transitions between satiated and socially deprived states
- Implications for understanding loneliness-related conditions in humans

The CC Forum brings together leading scientists to discuss frontier questions in life sciences. Dr. Liu's presentation was well received and sparked discussion on the translational relevance of circuit-level findings to human social health.
